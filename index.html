Pro Trading Tips

Master these fundamentals to boost your trading success

Risk Management First

Never risk more than 2% of your portfolio on a single trade. Set stop-losses before entering any position to protect your capital.

Follow the Trend

The trend is your friend. Use moving averages and trend lines to identify market direction and trade with the momentum.

Volume Confirms Price

High volume validates price movements. Look for increasing volume on breakouts and decreasing volume during consolidations.

Keep a Trading Journal

Document every trade with entry/exit reasons, emotions, and outcomes. Learn from both wins and losses to improve your strategy.

Patience Pays Off

Wait for high-probability setups that match your criteria. Quality over quantity - it's better to make fewer, better trades.

Control Your Emotions

Fear and greed are traders' worst enemies. Stick to your plan, use position sizing, and never chase losses with bigger bets.
